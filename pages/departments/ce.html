HOD Desk

Dr. M. G. Kalyanshetti Head of Department

It gives me immense pleasure to introduce our Civil Engineering Department, which is one of the oldest Department of the Institute.

The Strengths of our department are excellent Laboratories equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, qualified and experienced faculty having expertise in diverse fields, and excellent Industry Institute interaction in the form of consultancy services, training, sponsored projects, joint research work, etc. As a result of this, the Department has received the AICTE-CII award of ‘Best Industry Linked Institute in Civil Engineering’ for three years, i.e., 2013, 2014, and 2018.

The Department has been accredited four times by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. The department has signed an MOU with reputed organizations Like the National Highway Authority of India (NHAI), Progressive Engineering Consultants, Maheshwari Group, Indovance Pvt. Ltd, etc., for joint research, training, internship, and placement of students.

A Centre of Excellence (CoE) in association with UltraTech Cement Ltd. is established in the Department of Civil Engineering with a perspective of Industry-Institute interaction. This CoE strengthens the Industry-Institute Interaction, which will provide a common platform between Industry and Institute to plan and execute activities such as joint R&D work, industry-based joint consultancy, symposiums, workshops, students’ training, internships, and placements etc.

Department has received funds of @ 1.2 crores from AICTE and other agencies under various schemes like the Research Promotion Scheme (RPS), Modernization and removal of obsolesce (MODROBS), University seed money, etc. which enable us to develop excellent research laboratories.

The Department renders Consultancy services in the various domains of Civil Engineering like Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Surveying, Concrete Technology, and Environmental Engineering along with Proof consultancy services for the analysis and design of civil Engineering structures. We also undertake Third Party Technical Audit of several civil engineering works executed by PWD, SMC, Nagar Parishad, etc. This helps us to give more practical knowledge to the students with an encouragement to take industry-sponsored projects and several Industrial visits which enhance student learning.

Students are encouraged to participate in a Skill development program that includes the development of software proficiency and employability-focused Tests such as the Group Discussion/Personal Interview series, which are effectively conducted by the IEI (India) Student Club. The Civil Engineering students consistently perform well in Competitive examinations like GATE and Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) Technical Services.

Our Mission is to provide a learning experience that will develop students’ technical and professional skills to succeed in a wide range of careers contributing to the research and innovation, advancement of design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure to enhance the quality of life for humanity in a sustainable way.

Thank You.

About Department

The Department of Civil Engineering at Walchand Institute of Technology Solapur is established in the year 1987. The department offers a B.Tech in Civil Engineering (UG Course) with an Honors Degree in Infrastructure Engineering and Honors Degree in Sustainability Engineering, an M.Tech. in Civil-Structural Engineering (P.G. Course), and a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering (Doctoral Program).

The department has been Accredited four times by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) New Delhi along with an Institutional NAAC A+ grade. The Department has received a National Award for Best Industry-linked Technical Institute in Civil Engineering from AICTE-CII for three years i.e., 2013, 2014, and 2018.

The Department is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, Qualified and experienced faculty having expertise in diverse fields, and excellence in Industry Institute interaction.

The department has an excellent infrastructural facility which consists of about 13 Laboratories. Besides high-quality teaching at both UG and PG levels, the Department is actively involved in providing high-quality technical advisory support through various R&D projects and consultancy to various organizations. The Department has received funds of about 1.2 crores from AICTE and other agencies under various schemes like the Research Promotion Scheme (RPS), Modernization and removal of obsolesce (MODROBS), University seed money, etc. which enable us to develop excellent research laboratories. ‘The National Ambient Air Quality Monitoring Project’ sponsored by the Central Pollution Control Board of the Government of India has been run by the department from the 1st of January 1997 to till date. The department has signed an MOU with reputed organizations Like the National Highway Authority of India (NHAI), Progressive Engineering Consultants, Maheshwari Group, Indovance Pvt. Ltd, etc. for joint research, training, internship, and placement of students.

A Centre of Excellence (CoE) in association with UltraTech Cement Ltd. is established in the Department of Civil Engineering with a perspective of Industry-Institute interaction. This CoE strengthens the Industry-Institute Interaction, which will provide a common platform between Industry and Institute to plan and execute activities such as joint R&D work, industry-based joint consultancy, symposiums, workshops, students’ training, internships, and placements etc.

Apart from academics, the Department also encourages students to participate in extra-curricular and co-curricular activities, essential for the development of organizational skills. The department has prepared an academic calendar which has a detailed academic as well as other activities schedule. The department has IEI (India) Students Club which provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and also encourages them to participate in employability-focused Tests, Group discussions/Personal Interviews, field visits, etc. Seminars are arranged to improve the communication and presentation skills of students. Mini-project work undertaken by students has improved their problem-solving skills. Parent-teacher meetings are regularly arranged to interact with the parents, during which the student’s progress and departmental activities are discussed. The department has started a mentoring scheme in which every student meets their mentor faculty every fortnight to discuss issues related to academic, curricular, and other career opportunities.

Last year (2023-24), various companies selected about 23 students through campus interviews, with the highest package of 7.5 Lakhs PA. Placement for 2024-25 is in progress and 10 students are selected from various companies with an average package of 4.0 Lakhs PA.
