Dr. Mrs. M. A. Nirgude Head of Department

Dear Students, It is a privilege to reflect on the department’s sustained growth and accomplishments since its establishment in 1998.

Built on the collective commitment of our faculty, staff, students, alumni, and industry partners, the department continues to advance its mission of developing globally competent engineers in Computer Engineering and allied domains—professionals who are technically strong, ethically grounded, and committed to the sustainable development of society.

We prioritize quality, technology-enabled education that remains aligned with evolving societal needs and industry expectations. Contemporary teaching–learning practices such as active learning, experiential pedagogy, project-based learning, and blended learning are integrated with updated infrastructure and well equipped laboratories to create a rigorous and engaging academic environment. Our faculty members continuously strengthen their expertise through research, publications, professional certifications, and collaborations—ensuring that academic depth is complemented by real-world relevance. The department also has a strong track record of receiving AICTE and PAHSU (Solapur University) grants for organizing FDPs, conferences, workshops, and other capacity-building initiatives.

To prepare students for the rapidly transforming IT landscape, the department fosters lifelong learning and adaptability through exposure to emerging and high-impact areas such as Generative AI, Agentic AI, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, and Business Intelligence. Strong academia industry interaction through MoUs, internships, expert lectures, industry-oriented learning, and hands-on training strengthens student readiness and expands career opportunities.

Our students consistently demonstrate academic excellence, secure university ranks, and actively participate in technical, co-curricular, and extracurricular activities at national and international levels. As consistent winners at the Smart India Hackathon, they showcase critical thinking, creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving capability by designing solutions for real-world and societal challenges. Through student associations such as CSI and various departmental initiatives, we emphasize holistic development, leadership, communication skills, and responsible professional conduct. In addition, our students have secured on-campus placements in leading organizations such as HSBC, Cognizant, Infosys, TCS, Softinger, Capgemini, Deloitte, SAS (Data & AI Solutions), Pentagon Space Pvt. Ltd., and SmartRecon Technologies.

Innovation, research, and entrepreneurship are actively encouraged through the Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC), with a growing focus on sustainable, secure, and socially responsible solutions. The continued support of our alumni network, committed technical staff, and industry collaborators further enriches the department’s ecosystem and strengthens our ability to deliver meaningful outcomes.

As we move forward, we remain committed to nurturing capable, future-ready engineers who can innovate with purpose, adapt with confidence, and contribute responsibly to the digital and sustainable transformation of society.

About Department

This Programme was established in 1999. The Programme is equipped with latest computer machines, installed with latest softwares. We have ten computer laboratories which are well equipped with latest terminals & peripherals and legal copies of latest software’s. These laboratories support an environment for Networking, Multimedia, Databases and advanced technologies like Mobile Application Development , AI & Machine Learning, Data Science and one laboratory is oriented for basic study of electronics and microprocessor. We have 2 faculty completed Ph.D. and Four faculty pursuing PhD , Five PGs from reputed institutes like I.I.T, VTU Belgaum, Solapur University, S.R.T.M. University and Pune University. Faculty from the Programme have won Awards for their remarkable Research and contribution to Education Technologies enhancing the teaching learning process. Few faculty from the Programme are involved in Consultancy Activities. Information Technology Programme has been consistently bagging all five top university ranks of Solapur University for IT UG courses. We have been in a position to conduct three AICTE sponsored summer schools for effective teaching at national level. We have also conducted two national level workshops on Recent Trends in Cloud Computing and Recent Trends in Data Mining sponsored by AICTE and Solapur University.

We have received a grant of One Lac from AICTE for promoting innovation, creativity and Enterprenurship(SPICES).This year we have conducted a workshop entitled “ How to make your own Operating System” sponsored by Solapur University. We have signed MOUs with IBM, Cognizant, Infosys, RBTech & are accredited by TCS. We are also recognized by The Institute of Engineers (India). We are conducting value added programs for teachers at national level in collaboration with IIT Bombay of which we are a remote center. We have an active student’s chapter of computer Society of India (CSI) conducting workshops, Seminars, Guest Lectures and Industry Visit as academic calendar activities. Our students actively participate in online certification courses like NPTEL, MOOC, Infosys Springboard and Spoken Tutorial conducted by IITs. Our Programme Personality Development Cell helps to improve the communication skills, Professional Ethics, Aptitude and personality of our students. It is managed by our students and faculty of the Programme. Our teachers as well as students present Technical papers at State, National and International level competitions on a regular basis.

Our faculty and students publish their research in international journals. Vision & Mission

👁️

Vision

To produce globally competent engineers in Computer Engineering and allied fields, who will aim at overall sustainable development of the society.

🎓

Mission

  • M1: To impart quality education in Computer Engineering and allied fields in accordance with the needs of the society through technology enabled education.
  • M2: To inculcate lifelong learning in students to face challenges posed by ever-changing IT career landscape as a disciplined professional with a sense of professional ethics.
  • M3: To inculcate critical thinking and creativity for identifying various societal issues and to provide solutions.
  • M4: To enhance career opportunities for students through academia- industry interaction and research.
