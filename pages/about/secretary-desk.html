A message from our Honorary Secretary on our academic vision and trust legacy.

Dr. Ranjeet Gandhi

Hon. Secretary

'WIT the Knowledge Hub'

SAPD Jain Pathshala Trust has been continuously contributing to nation building through empowering generations with quality education since 1885. SAPD Jain Pathshala is taking new strides in the domain of Education with a futuristic vision and meeting the challenges of the globalized world and the disruptive innovations in education and various fields.

Walchand Institute of Technology is established to create, nurture, and shape technical professionals and leaders to create an inclusive and sustainable society from a national and international perspective. SAPD Jain Pathshala through its motto ‘Education is Religion’ has been upholding India’s rich heritage and at the same time embracing new disruptive and innovative ideas.

Pathshala aims to develop industry ready students with a strong value system and personality who will cater to the needs of society.

Dr. Ranjeet Gandhi

Hon. Secretary,
