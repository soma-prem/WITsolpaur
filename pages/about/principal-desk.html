WIT’s Academic Leadership

Right from its inception in 1983, Walchand Institute of Technology (WIT) is on its fast track of growth and academic contribution. WIT surged ahead purely due to its crystal clear vision, mission and commitment of the staff ensuring that we continue to shape our students to higher quality professionals; who are competent in their chosen field, follow morals and ethics and contribute to the development of our society.

WIT has State of the Art infrastructure with the best of the faculty to ensure the transformation of our students to world class professionals. The testimony to this is the quality certification of the college by NAAC, NBA and Autonomy! We are looking forward to developing in each of our faculty, staff, and student, the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively and, effectively for the betterment of mankind.

I welcome you to be a part of WIT to usher in an era of paradise on earth.

Dr. Sachin Ratikant Gengaje

Incharge Principal,
Walchand Institute of Technology, Solapur
